
This mod aims to add monster hunter armors into the game and has a skill point system similar to MHW-Rise, even vanilla armors get skills but you can turn that off in mod config if you wish.

There's no wiki right now so feel free to join the discord to ask questions, give suggestions or report issues and let me know what you want to see added!

Mod incompatibilities:
Any mods that messes with accessory slots will break the decorations.

Notable Bugs:
Quick equipping accessories will always get sent to the last slot.

The mod has not been tested in multiplayer.
